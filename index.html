Resume

Places I've been and things I've done.


After receiving a diploma for graphic design and being introduced to some html and css I knew I wanted to jump into the world of web design.

I got my foot in the door at a marketing and design firm, and worked my way up to junior web designer, heres how i got there.

I started in a design assistant position, helping client with thier needs directly related to the design and content of thier website and worked closely with the designers to bring the clients websites to life.

After a few months I walked up to the design director and asked if she would give me a shot, well she did and I was no longer a design assistant, and aspiring junior designer, ready to take on new challenges.

I spent hours and long friday nights digesting as much information as I could, and desperately throwing myself at any designer who would show me a thing or two.

In the following years, I've been recognized for being efficient,reliable and always bringing fresh new ideas to the table.
